Chicken Alfredo


Description

Chicken Alfredo is a pasta dish with cream sauce and chicken addedin with the pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast tenders
  • 1 pound uncooked pasta, such as rotini
  • 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, cubed
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 teaspoons McCormick® Roasted Garlic
  • 1 teaspoon McCormick® Whole Thyme Leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Coarse Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Place chicken in single layer on bottom of 13x9-inch baking dish sprayed with no stick cooking spray. Top with uncooked pasta and cream cheese. Mix milk, sour cream, flour, 1 cup mozzarella, 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, and spices in bowl until well mixed. Pour into baking dish over pasta. Top with remaining Parmesan and mozzarella cheeses. Cover with foil. Bake 30 minutes.
  2. Remove foil and bake an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is lightly browned.
